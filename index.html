About GMCO

Leading MEP Contractor in the United Arab Emirates

Established in 2011, GMCO is a leading MEP contracting company based in the United Arab Emirates, delivering comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing solutions across a wide range of sectors. With a strong reputation for quality and reliability, GMCO has successfully contributed to landmark projects in the commercial, residential, hospitality, healthcare, and industrial fields.

With more than a decade of industry experience, GMCO has built a solid portfolio of projects that reflect technical excellence, timely delivery, and strict adherence to international standards. Our expertise covers the full MEP lifecycle — from design coordination and value engineering to installation, testing, commissioning, and maintenance.
